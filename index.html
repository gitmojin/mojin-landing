About

An AI-augmented game studio

Small team, lots of agents, building games at the intersection of artificial intelligence and play.

Mojin is an indie game studio based in Sweden. We design and ship small, lovable games — and the tools that help us make them faster. Everything we build is shaped by the same question: what becomes possible when one person and a room full of AI agents can build like a full studio?

The games

We're shipping a growing library of small games — card games, puzzles, arcade action, and a few stranger experiments at the edges. The lineup leans toward short sessions, easy-to-learn rules, and designs that lend themselves well to playing together. New ones land regularly.

The community

Our portal is built around small crews — friends playing the same handful of games, chasing scores, swapping reviews. We're keeping things deliberately tight while we learn what works. The goal isn't a feed; it's a clubhouse.
